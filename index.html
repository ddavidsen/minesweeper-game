Charity: Water Minesweeper

The water is divided into cells, with contaminants randomly distributed. To win, you need to open all the cells. The number on a cell shows the number of contaminants adjacent to it. Using this information, you can determine cells that are safe, and cells that are not. Cells suspected of being contaminants can be marked with a flag using the right mouse button.
